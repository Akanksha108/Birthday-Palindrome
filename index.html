Check out if your Birthday is Palindrome

A palindrome is a word, sentence, verse, or even number that reads the same backward or forward. It derives from Greek roots that literally mean “running back”

Enter your birthdate and we will tell you if your birthdate is a palindrome

This app checks your birthdate in 4 formats: yyyy-mm-dd, dd-mm-yyyy, mm-dd-yy, m-dd-yyy

e.g.If your birthdate is 01 Aug,1995, then app will check for 19950801, 01081995, 080195,1081995
